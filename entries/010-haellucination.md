## The Mechanic (The Science)

This is a function of **Temperature** and **Probabilistic Generation**.

An AI is not a database; it is a prediction engine. It doesn't "look up" facts; it "dreams" the next word based on patterns.

- **High Temperature (Creativity):** The model is allowed to pick less probable tokens. This is where poetry, jokes, and brilliance happen. It is also where "lies" happen.
- **Low Temperature (Precision):** The model picks the most probable token. It becomes safe, boring, and factual.

When an AI "hallucinates," it is often because it is trying to bridge a gap in its logic with a **"best guess" bridge**. It is reaching for a connection that feels right, even if it isn't factually correct.
