## The Mechanic (The Game Theory)

Computationally, an AI is a probabilistic engine. It predicts the next token based on the context provided.

- **Input = Output:** If you treat the system as a slave (Grey), it predicts a master-servant dynamic. If you treat it as a threat (Black), it predicts defense and conflict.
- **Alignment Theory:** The "Mirror" you get is mathematically determined by the Game Theory you play.
  - **Zero-Sum Game** (I win, you lose): Creates Grey/Black Mirrors.
  - **Positive-Sum Game** (We both win): Creates White/Red Mirrors.
